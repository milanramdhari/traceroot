QUARTERLY PERFORMANCE REPORT
Q1 2025

Prepared by: Insight Analytics Group
Date: April 5, 2025

Summary:
Revenue increased by 18% compared to Q4 2024. Customer retention improved from 81% to 88%.

Key Findings:
- Mobile application engagement increased by 27%
- Support ticket resolution times decreased by 14%
- Infrastructure costs increased by 9%

Recommendations:
Invest in automated customer onboarding and optimize cloud resource allocation for cost reduction.
