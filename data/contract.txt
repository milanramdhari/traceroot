SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT AGREEMENT

This Software Development Agreement is entered into on February 10, 2025 between Orion Labs LLC and Velocity Commerce Inc.

Scope of Work:
Orion Labs LLC agrees to develop a custom inventory management platform for Velocity Commerce Inc.

Project Timeline:
Start Date: February 15, 2025
Completion Date: August 30, 2025

Compensation:
Velocity Commerce Inc. agrees to pay a total of $85,000 in milestone-based payments.

Confidentiality:
Both parties agree not to disclose confidential business information shared during the project.

Termination:
Either party may terminate this agreement with 14 days written notice.
